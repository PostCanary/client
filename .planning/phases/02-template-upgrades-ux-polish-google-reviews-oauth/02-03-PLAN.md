## Google Places TOS compliance (drake-memory ID 99)

**What's compliant for print:**
- Aggregate rating (4.9★) + review count (2423 reviews) — these are the business's own facts
- The business CLAIMING "4.9★ rated" is their own marketing claim

**What's NOT compliant for print:**
- Individual review text copied from Google Places API → printed on postcard
- Reviewer names from Google Places API → printed on postcard
- Google's star visualization/branding

**For demo:** The testimonial is a written placeholder, not sourced from Google. Clearly documented.
**For production:** Reviews must come from customer-submitted testimonials OR with explicit customer permission, NOT scraped from Google.
