Drake caught it with "will they be identical copies to the ones on that website you got them from? can we even use those?" — which is exactly the question Claude should have asked itself before proposing the approach. **What structural mechanism prevents it?** The V1 spec at lines 968-973 explicitly prohibits copying: *"Templates are PostCanary ORIGINALS based on universal design principles. NOT copied from PostcardMania, Modern Postcard, or any competitor."* The discipline: extract PATTERNS (abstract rules, not copyrightable) from multiple references, then build from the pattern list with references CLOSED. `02-RESEARCH.md` § "Universal Patterns" IS the legal abstraction layer.
